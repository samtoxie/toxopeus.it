About Me

I'm Sam, I'm a dutch software engineer currently studying at Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. As part of the programme we create and maintain (web-)applications and informationsystems.

I also like to enjoy nature's beauty. Whether it is with my dog or with my camera, it is a great way for me to clear my mind and relieve some stress.
